# Real-Estate-Performance
Analyzing sales trends, buyer insights, and growth opportunities
## INTRODUCTION
This analysis provides a data-driven overview of real estate performance from 2020-2025.
Tracks revenue growth, transaction volumes, average sale price, and buyer base.
Highlights market trends and areas of strength.
Offers an outlook for 2025, including buyer opportunities still in the pipeline.
Built using SQL (for data preparation and analysis) and Power BI (for visualization and insights).
## INSIGHTS
REVENUE GROWTH
Sales increased from $5.8M (2020) to $7.6M (2024).
This shows sustained growth momentum and strong performance in property sale. It represents a 31% growth in 5 years.

STABLE TRANSACTION VOLUME WITH 2024 PEAK
Annual transaction volume hovered between 12 and 15.
2024 hit 15 deals, the highest in 5 years, showing peak productivity by the sales team.

 AVERAGE SALE PRICE GROWTH
Average property sale price increased from $448K (2020) to $509K+ (2024). This indicates a shift toward premium property sales.

BUYER BASE
Consistent 10-14 unique buyers yearly.
Peak of 14 buyers in 2021 ( strong client acquisition)

## RECOMMENDATION

Convert Potential Buyers. Assign agents personally to follow up.
Launch a referral program to gain new clients through satisfied buyers.
Expand listings in the $500K+ range.
Maintain 15+ deals annually (2024 benchmark).
Prioritize conversion of the 9 interested buyers still in the market.
Break sales goals into quarterly targets  to track progress and reduce year-end pressure.

## CONCLUSION
The company has achieved steady revenue growth, rising property values, and a consistent buyer base. 
With strong potential buyers still in the pipeline and growing demand for premium properties, the outlook remains highly positive. 
Strategic follow-ups, stronger marketing, and buyer retention initiatives will drive continued success.
